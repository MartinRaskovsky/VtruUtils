VaWa App - Doc’s Vault & Wallet Tracker App

Tracking your assets across multiple wallets and networks can be time-consuming and frustrating. Most blockchain explorers only allow checking one wallet at a time, making it difficult to get a complete picture of your holdings—especially when following best practices like spreading assets across multiple wallets for security.

VaWa was created to solve this challenge.

Originally designed for Vitruveo, the app was extended to support SEVO-X on Binance Smart Chain (BSC), enabling users to track both Vitruveo and SEVO-X assets in a single interface. With this multi-network expansion, VaWa now supports Vitruveo (VTRU), Ethereum (ETH) and Binance Coin (BNB).

What is VaWa?

VaWa (Vault and Wallet Tracker) is a multi-network, multi-wallet tool built for Vitruveo users who want a unified view of their assets. Instead of manually checking one address at a time, VaWa lets you track all your wallets and networks in one place.

Key Features

  • View details of your Vitruveo Vault and its linked wallets together with as many extra wallets.
  • Track all your Vitruveo tokens and coins across multiple wallets in a single dashboard.
  • Find out exactly Where are my tokens? without checking multiple explorers.
  • Monitor SEVO-X on BSC alongside your Vitruveo holdings.
  • Support for multiple networks:
    • VTRU on Vitruveo, along with staked VTRU, VERSE, VIBE, and VORTEX.
    • BNB on Binance Smart Chain (BSC) and staked SEVO-X.
    • ETH on Ethereum for Ethereum-based balances.

Why VaWa?

Manually piecing together wallet balances across multiple explorers is inefficient and frustrating. VaWa was designed to solve that problem.

  • Developed primarily for Vitruveo, ensuring a full overview of your Vault and wallet balances.
  • Integrates SEVO-X on BSC, tracking Vitruveo-related assets across chains.
  • Supports Vitruveo, Ethereum and BNB, allowing you to see your entire portfolio in one place.

Instead of using separate blockchain explorers for Vitruveo, BSC and Ethereum, VaWa unifies everything into one convenient and efficient platform.
