VaWa App - Doc’s Vault & Wallet Tracker

Tracking your assets across multiple wallets and networks can be time-consuming and frustrating. Most blockchain explorers only allow checking one wallet at a time, making it difficult to get a complete picture of your holdings—especially when following best practices like spreading assets across multiple wallets for security.

VaWa was created to solve this challenge.

Originally designed for Vitruveo, VaWa was later extended to support SEVO-X on Binance Smart Chain (BSC), enabling users to track Vitruveo and SEVO-X assets in a single interface. VaWa now supports Vitruveo (VTRU), Ethereum (ETH), and Binance Coin (BNB).

What is VaWa?

VaWa (Vault and Wallet Tracker) is a multi-network, multi-wallet tool that provides a unified view of your assets, eliminating the need to check multiple blockchain explorers individually.

Key Features

  • View details of your Vitruveo Vault and linked wallets.
  • Track all your Vitruveo tokens and coins across multiple wallets in a single dashboard.
  • Find out exactly Where are my tokens? without checking multiple explorers.
  • Discover hidden or forgotten tokens and coins.
  • Monitor SEVO-X on BSC alongside your Vitruveo holdings.
  • Supports multiple networks:
    • Native Coins: VTRU, ETH, BNB, and POL.
    • VTRU Bridged: VTRU on BSC and ETH.
    • USDC: Tracked across VTRU, ETH, BSC, and POL networks.
    • Staked: VTRU Staked and SEVO-X Staked on BSC.
    • Vitruveo Coins & Tokens: VERSE, VIBE, VORTEX, VTRO, VUSD, and wVTRU.
    • Vitruveo Exchange: V3DEX and VITDEX.

Why VaWa?

Manually piecing together wallet balances across multiple explorers is inefficient and frustrating. VaWa was designed to simplify this process.

  • Developed for Vitruveo, ensuring a complete overview of Vault and wallet balances.
  • Integrates SEVO-X on BSC for cross-chain asset tracking.
  • Supports Vitruveo, Ethereum, and BNB, providing a unified portfolio view.

Instead of using separate blockchain explorers, VaWa unifies everything into one convenient platform.
